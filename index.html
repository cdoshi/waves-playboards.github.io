What is a playboard/busyboard?

A playboard/busyboard is a safe play area for the child to explore the unusual objects on it. The uniqueness of the objects help fulfill the child's curiosity for it. Moreover a child's excess energy is channelized while playing with the objects. A customized playboard turns into your child's play-buddy at home as the child explores and interacts with it comfortably. The board features a variety of components that children can manipulate, touch, and explore, such as locks, latches, zippers, buttons, switches, knobs, wheels, mirrors, bells, and more.
