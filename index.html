What is a playboard/busyboard?

A customized playboard turns into a play-buddy as the child explores and interacts with it comfortably. Select the objects to be added from a variety of components like latches, zippers, buttons, switches, knobs, wheels, mirrors, bells, and more. The uniqueness of the objects and a variety of components give access to children to manipulate, touch, and explore.

A playboard is suitable for personal use or institutions with toddlers presence like preschools, schools, indoor play areas, pediatric centers, etc.
